_**Dark & Wild**_ (stylized in all caps) is the first studio album by BTS. It was released on August 20, 2014, with "Danger" serving as the album's title track. "호르몬 전쟁 (War of Hormone)" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. 

The album was the fourteenth best-selling album of 2014 in South Korea.

## Trivias[]

  * _Danger_ was released on August 30, 2014, at 12:00am, in Korea, however in the UK it released on August 19, 2014, at 15:00pm (3:00p m).
  * _War of Hormone_ was released on October 22, 2014 at 12:00a m, in Korea, however in the UK it released on October 21, 2014, at 15:00p m (3:00p m).
  * The Title track of the album is _Danger_ , which seems to be the sequel of _Boy In Luv_.
  * In Taiwan Special Limited Edition, they've released a Chinese version of _Boy In Luv_. It is their only one Chinese song in the whole discography of BTS.
