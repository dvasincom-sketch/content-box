_**Map of the Soul: 7**_ is the fourth studio album by BTS. It was released on February 21, 2020, with "ON" serving as the album's title track. "Black Swan" is a pre-release track and was also used as a follow-up track during promotions. 

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's fourth #1 on that chart. 

This album was the best-selling album of 2020 in South Korea and the world. This album is currently the best-selling album in South Korea of all time. 

As January 2023, the album has sold 5,022,205 copies only in South Korea.

## Records[]

  * The album reached 3,420,000 copies of Pre-orders, beating their previous record by almost 700,000 orders, with their mini-album, Map of the Soul: Persona, with 2,680,000 pre-orders.

## Trivia[]

  * Map of the Soul: 7 has the highest number of tracks on an album, with 19 tracks on the physical album and 20 on the digital version excluding compilation albums.
  * This beats out Skool Luv Affair Special Addition and You Never Walk Alone which both had 18 tracks.
