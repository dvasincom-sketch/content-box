## Development[]

On November 11, 2014, Big Hit Entertainment and CJ E&M released their Webtoon series, titled "Hip Hop Monster", featuring BTS as cartoon characters. The characters were created based on the members' personalities and interests, and they were featured in various merchandise and promotional materials. The group's second mini album _Skool Luv Affair_ indeed included stickers of these characters.

## Merchandise[]

The brand's products were initially released in two batches and were available for purchase in several South Korean online and offline stores. The first batch was released on November 11, 2014 and the second batch on November 12, 2014. The merchandise included: 

  * Figurines
  * Mirrors
  * Mouse pads
  * Flash drives
  * Stickers
  * Notebooks
  * Bottles
  * Bags
  * Plush dolls
