_**The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.2**_ (화양연화 Pt.2; 花樣年華 Pt.2) is the fourth mini album by BTS. It was released on November 30, 2015 with "RUN" serving as the album's title track. "Butterfly" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. 

The album was the fifth best-selling album of 2015 in South Korea.
