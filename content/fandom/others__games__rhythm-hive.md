**Rhythm Hive** (리듬하이브, stylized in all caps; **RHYTHM HIVE**) is a rhythm game developed by HYBE IM (former Superb Corp), featuring songs by BTS, TXT, ENHYPEN, SEVENTEEN, LE SSERAFIM, NewJeans, BOYNEXTDOOR, ILLIT and TWS. The game was launched worldwide on the App Store and Google Play on February 4, 2021.

## Gameplay[]

### Game Modes[]

Much like its predecessor, SuperStar BTS, Rhythm Hive has different game modes to play in, with the addition of music catalog of TXT, ENHYPEN, SEVENTEEN, LE SSERAFIM, NewJeans and BOYNEXTDOOR. 

  * **Chapter Stage** \- Chapters are multiple mission stages with a songs of any group with various requirements to clear (eg. score, consecutive hits, miss limit, etc) for rewards. There are currently three chapters.
  * **Studio** \- Single-play mode. Songs can be played in Easy, Normal, Hard or Super Hard mode and in Short or Full durations.

#### Restricted Modes[]

  * **Live Performance** \- Compete against other players by gaining the most points in a song. You can pick one members card and go against other players who had picked other members. This game mode was temporarily restricted in the June 15, 2023 update.

#### Former Modes[]

  * **Mix Challenge** \- In this mode, you create a mix of three songs with different difficulty parameters that you create and other plays can play for rewards. You can also play other players' mixes. This game mode was removed in the February 7, 2022 update.
  * **Cheer Mode** \- This mode is a new type of stage that engages in real cheering for each artist's songs. Tap the screen to the timing of the Squad Leader’s movements to progress through the stage. Cheer Theme Performace cards increase the final overal score.

### Performance Cards[]

**Performance Cards** are collectibles that can be equipped to obtain higher scores in game modes. Each card features a different member of the selected group with varying themes and are bound to selected songs. Cards can be obtained as rewards for completing various quests and activities, or through 'gatcha-style' card draws for premium currency.
