_**FACE**_ is the debut mini album by Jimin. It was released on March 24, 2023 with "Like Crazy" serving as the album's title track. A pre-release track "Set Me Free Pt.2" was released on March 17, 2023. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
