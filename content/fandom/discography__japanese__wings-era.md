_**FACE YOURSELF**_ is the third Japanese studio album by BTS. It was released on April 4, 2018. The album features the singles "Blood Sweat & Tears" and "MIC Drop/DNA/Crystal Snow" and new original Japanese songs. 

The album is certified Double Platinum by RIAJ for the shipment of 500,000 copies.
