_**Love Yourself: Answer**_ (LOVE YOURSELF 結 'Answer') is the second special album by BTS. It was released on August 24, 2018 with "IDOL" serving as the album's title track. "I'm Fine" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. The album contains the tracks from their two previous albums, seven new songs and three new remixes. 

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's second #1 on that chart. 

The album was the best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea and second best-selling album of 2018 in the world. This album is currently the fifth best-selling album on South Korea of all time. 

As 2022, the album has sold 3,329,846 copies only in South Korea.
