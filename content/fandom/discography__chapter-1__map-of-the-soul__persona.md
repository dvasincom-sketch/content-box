_**Map of the Soul: Persona**_ is the sixth mini album by BTS. It was released on April 12, 2019 with "작은 것들을 위한 시 (Boy With Luv) (feat. Halsey)" serving as the album's title track. "Dionysus" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. 

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's third #1 on that chart. 

This album was the best-selling album of 2019 in South Korean history and third best-selling album of 2019 in the world. This album is currently the second best-selling album in South Korea of all time, with _MAP OF THE SOUL: 7_ being number one. 

As January 2023, the album has sold 4,616,938 copies only in South Korea.

## Records[]

  * The album reached 2,680,000 copies of pre-orders, beating their previous record by almost 1,100,000 orders, with their compilation album, Love Yourself: Answer, with 1,511,910 pre-orders.
  * _Map Of The Soul: Persona_ breaks their 1st Week Hanteo Sales Record, in just one day, with 1,479,930 sales. With their previous record being, Love Yourself: Tear selling 1,003,527 units on Hanteo in its first week.
