_**D-DAY**_ is the debut studio album by Agust D. It was released on April 21, 2023 with "Haegeum" serving as the album's title track. A pre-release track "People, Pt. 2 (feat. IU)" was released on April 7, 2023. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
