The **BTS Universe** or **Bangtan Universe** (often shortened to **BU** ; 방탄소년단 세계관), initially known as **The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Series** , **Hwa Yang Yeon Hwa Series** or **HYYH Series** until the official name and logo were first used in 2017, is an original story that is centered on a series of music videos and short films, independently produced by HYBE and Big Hit Music, and based on characters played by the members of BTS. The franchise has expanded to include short stories, books, a webtoon, a visual novel and a web drama.

## Development[]

On April 29, 2015, BTS began their second era with a mini album in which the song "I Need U" served as the album's title track. The music video for this track shows a story of the seven members in an alternate reality, depicting their anxieties and uncertainties as they confront their futures. Following this music video story, multiple content were released in this era and in their third era. Due to the popularity of the fictional universe, Big Hit Entertainment named it the "BU", or "BTS Universe". 

On June 29, 2017, the BTS Wings Concept Book, the first content with the official BTS Universe logo, was released. In an interview from July 2017, RM explains the origin of their "I Need U" and "Run" music videos by saying: "_Stars Wars_ came out tens of years ago, but a dad and his son still go to a theater to see [it]. It’s not just like a five-year or a ten-year thing, you know what I mean? Our company knew that and they always told us about how important it is to make a world like  _Star Wars_ or _Marvel_ [did]." In August 2017, at the start of their fourth era, posters of the fictional characters were released. At the same time, the phrase "BU content certified by Big Hit Entertainment" was added to all music videos and short films that were part of the BU. 

Since the BU was established, _BE_ was the first album without any content related to the fictional universe, on BE-hind Story released on December 2020, Jin explains "[_BE_] It wasn't part of BTS Universe and it was really natural so I think it felt more comfortable. We can't forget about the universe but we should make albums like this to take a break once in a while where we all participate together." 

## History[]

"Seven boys. Best friends. Their fates intertwined through the good times together, but also the tough times, as they have gone their separate ways and suffered greatly as a result. When all is almost lost for these boys, one is given a special chance to go back in time and help his friends fix the mistakes that led them down this path. He’ll do anything to save them, but can he? Or is he too late?" – Plot summary of the _花樣年華 Pt.0<SAVE ME>_ webtoon. 

## Locations[]

_See also:Songju_

BU mainly occurs in Songju a fictional city in South Korea. The map of Songju was first seen in the last pages of each version in _花樣年華 THE NOTES 1_. 

In the museum HYBE Insight a map of Songju along with connections as objects and events can be found.

1/5

On August 9, 2017, Jin posted on Twitter a selca featuring a bouquet of flowers and the word "Smeraldo", which is not an actual flower. However, the fans found Testesso, a fictional florist who is planning on opening a shop called _Smeraldo Flower Shop_. 

Testesso has a Naver Blog about the Smeraldo Flower and his shop, with different stories about the origin of the flower and his experiences with it. These stories were deleted and the BU logo was added to the blog on July 4, 2018. A few days later, the blog started to post the same stories as last year, but the eighth story changed and Seok-jin didn't pre-order the _Smeraldo Flowers_ for the girl because he turned back the time. 

Also, the Instagram account associated with the blog was deleted and re-opened on July 20, 2018, with content alike to previously uploaded and the Twitter account was private since August 2017, until the content was deleted and reupdated on July 2018. 

As of July 6, 2020, all the content of the blog was deleted for the second time with posts slowly being reuploaded again on the same dates. However, the third story changes and gets uploaded on July 15, 2020 instead of July 17, 2020. In the previous versions, Testesso talks about how the flower was first discovered in real life. On the 2020 version, he talks about seeing a man with an umbrella stopping in front of the shop during the typhoon. Afterwards, the uploads proceed just as before. 

*Don't confuse Jin, the vocalist of BTS with Seok-jin, the fictional character.

### Smeraldo Books' Twitter[]

On January 6, 2019, a Twitter account promoting the _花樣年華 Pt.0<SAVE ME>_ webtoon, the _花樣年華 THE NOTES 1_ book and the _花樣年華 THE NOTES 2_ book was opened. The account shared fragments of the books tied with different visual content on the BU. 

On July 29, 2020, the account started polls, giving the fans the chance to decide what happens next, the polls finished on August 4, 2020. A day before it ended, a new hashtag was added to promote the BU video game as well.

### Other Original Stories[]

  * **7Fates: CHAKHO** : CHAKHO is the second original story featuring BTS, it started with a webtoon and webnovel in January 15, 2021. It is an urban fantasy story inspired by the tiger hunters of Joseon dynasty known as "Chakhogapsa." The CHAKHO's characters names are the same as the alternative names of BTS' characters in the BEGINS ≠ YOUTH web drama.

  * **THE STAR SEEKERS** : Formerly known as TXT Universe, TU and +U, started on January 11, 2019, is an original story that included in music videos and photocards, also produced by HYBE. Similarly to the BU, it is based on characters inspired by the members of TXT. Although the BU and the TSS are different universes, the cat-like creature with one blue eye and one green eye who meets Seok-jin at the beach, Jung-kook after his accident and Eugene in several places travel in between both universes looking for his own universe.

  * **DARK MOON** : Unlike the THE STAR SEEKERS, there are no as-direct references that DARK MOON has with the BU. 
    * **DARK MOON: THE BLOOD ALTAR** : It is an original story of ENHYPEN which formally got its name in "Drunk-Dazed" teaser in April 21, 2021 and is created by HYBE's sublabel Belift Lab. The characters are based around the members of the group similar to the BU and THE STAR SEEKERS. Content that are officially part of the DARK MOON universe are tagged with "DARK MOON with ENHYPEN," such as the music videos of "Drunk-Dazed" and "FEVER".
    * **DARK MOON: THE GREY CITY** : It is an original story of &TEAM, started with "Under the skin" music video on November 21, 2022 and is created by HYBE's sublabel HYBE Labels Japan. The characters are based around the members of the group similar to the BU and THE STAR SEEKERS.
    * **DARK MOON: TWO MOONS** : The second original story of ENHYPEN, it started with 'ORANGE BLOOD' concept trailer in October 26, 2023.
    * **DARK MOON: CHILDREN OF VAMFIELD** : The third of original story of ENHYPEN, it started with a webtoon with the same name in March 17, 2024.
    * **DARK MOON: THE BLOOD OF VARGR** : Upcoming original story of ENHYPEN.

  * **Crimson Heart:** It is an original story of LE SSERAFIM, started with "Blue Flame" song on May 2, 2022 and is created by HYBE's sublabel Source Music. The characters are based around the members of the group similar to the BU, 7FATES: CHAKHO, THE STAR SEEKERS and DARK MOON.

  * **SUMMER MOON: THE QUPRIDS** : It is an original story of ILLIT, started with "Magnetic" music video on March 25, 2024.

  * **GFRIEND's Original Story:** In July 2019, HYBE has acquired Source Music an entertainment company, who manages the girl group GFRIEND, making the group part of the multi-label organization, HYBE Labels. On the corporate briefing of the second half of 2019, Bang Si-hyuk said "Storytelling will be expanded in stages to include artists in our multi-label organization." The short film _A Tale of the Glass Bead : Previous Story_ was released explaining the GFRIEND music videos storytelling, but so far is not established as an official universe. On May 18, 2021 GFRIEND was confirmed to be parting ways with their agency Source Music after all of the members decided not to renew their contracts.

  * **HYBE Labels's Artists Original Stories** : Multiple artists as the groups SEVENTEEN, New Jeans, BOYNEXTDOOR, TWS, KATSEYE, aoen, CORTIS, SANTOS BRAVOS, the soloists Lee Hyun, Bumzu, Zico, Hwang Min Hyun and Heeseung are part of the multi-label organization but so far none of the other acts has an original story.

## Cultural references[]

  * The book _Demian_ by Hermann Hesse is quoted in the _WINGS Short Films_ and _花樣年華 The Notes 1_ , meanwhile the "Blood Sweat & Tears" music video uses a lot of objects and elements of the book. Also, the book appears in BEGINS ≠ YOUTH drama.
  * The paintings _The Fall of the Rebel Angels_ and _Landscape with the Fall of Icarus_ by Pieter Bruegel the Elder, and the ceiling in Ljubljana Cathedral by Giulio Quaglio the Younger appears in "Blood Sweat & Tears" music video and "血、汗、涙 -Japanese Ver.-" music video.
  * The sculptures _Venus de Milo,_ _Laocoön and His Sons_ (only Laocoön's bust), Livia attired as the goddess Ops, _Crouching Venus,_ _Perseus with the Head of Medusa,_ _Ares Borghese,_ _Pietà,_ and the painting _The Lament for Icarus_ by Herbert James Draper appears in "Blood Sweat & Tears" music video.
  * The quote "'Magic Shop' is a psychodramatic technique that exchanges fear for a positive attitude." in _'FAKE LOVE' Official Teaser 1_ is a reference to the book _Into the Magic Shop_ by James Doty.
  * The painting _The Raft of the Medusa_ by Théodore Géricault appears in BTS Universe Story Official Concept Art Teaser and it is mentioned in _花樣年華 The Notes_ in _MAP OF THE SOUL : PERSONA_ and _花樣年華 The Notes 2._
  * A quote from _The Art of Loving_ by Erich Fromm can be found in _花樣年華 The Notes 1_.
  * The book _Cosmos_ by Carl Sagan is mentioned in BTS Universe Story.
