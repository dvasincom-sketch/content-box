**BTS 7 Moments** is a video archive and photobook package showcasing BTS's activities through 2022 and 2023 as a group and as solo artists. It will be released on April 2, 2025.

  * 2 Contents
  * 3 Videos

## Background[]

On March 17, 2025, a 3D visualizer was released regarding the package. Further information on the package was released on March 18, 2025. 

## Contents[]

  1. Outbox (116x165x46mm)
  2. Photobook (155x105mm / 180p)
  3. Photocard Set (55x85mm / 7ea 1set)
  4. Digital Code Set 
     * Tin Case (88x55mm)
     * Digital Code (30x40mm / approximately 66 minutes)
     * Silver Chain (14cm)
     * Paper (70x40mm)
  5. Instructions (90x140mm)
  6. Sticker Set 
     * Sticker Envelope (90x95mm)
     * Sticker (80x80mm / 4ea 1set)

  * Pre-order: March 19, 2025
  * Release date: April 2, 2025 (Global); April 17, 2025 (Japan); April 30, 2025 (USA)
