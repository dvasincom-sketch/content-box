_**O!RUL8,2?**_ _(Oh! Are you late, too?)_ is the first mini album by BTS. It was released on September 11, 2013 with "N.O" serving as the album's title track.

## Trivias[]

  * "N.O" was released on September 12, 2013 at 12:00 am, in Korea, however in the UK it was released on September 11, 2013, at 15:00 (3:00 pm).
  * "N.O" is an acronym word for "No Offense".
