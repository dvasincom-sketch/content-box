**HOPE ON THE STREET VOL.1** is the first soundtrack album by j-hope for his documentary film HOPE ON THE STREET. It was released on March 29, 2024 with "NEURON (with Gaeko, yoonmirae)" serving as the album's title track. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
