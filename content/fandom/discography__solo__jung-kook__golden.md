_**GOLDEN**_ is the debut studio album by Jung Kook. It was released on November 3, 2023 with "Standing Next To You" serving as the album's title track. Two pre-release tracks "Seven (feat. Latto)" and "3D (feat. Jack Harlow)" were released on July 14, 2023 and September 29, 2023. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
