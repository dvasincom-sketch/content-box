_**Right Place, Wrong Person**_ is the second studio album by RM. It was released on May 24, 2024 with "LOST!" serving as the album's title track. A pre-release track "Come back to me" was released on May 10, 2024. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
