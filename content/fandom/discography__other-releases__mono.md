_**Mono**_ (stylized as **mono.**) is the second mixtape (and a playlist) by RM. It was released on October 23, 2018 with "Forever Rain" serving as the mixtape's title track. 

The mixtape topped the iTunes Top Albums Charts in a total of 92 countries and regions setting the record with the most #1 spots on the chart by a Korean artist. 

It was released officially on November 29, 2022 on all digital music platforms.
