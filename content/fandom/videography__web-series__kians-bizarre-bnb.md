**Kian's Bizarre B &B** (대환장 기안장) is a variety show featuring Kian84, Jin and Ji Ye-eun. It was released weekly for three weeks since April 8, 2025 until April 22, 2025 and broadcasting on Netflix. 

In the last episode aired on April 22, 2025 a season two was announced. The production of the second season began on July 10, 2025. On February 26, 2026, Netflix confirmed that Jin won't come back for the season two.
