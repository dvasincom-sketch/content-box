_**Proof**_ (stylized in all caps) is the first anthology album by BTS. It was released on June 10, 2022 with "Yet To Come (The Most Beautiful Moment)" serving as the album's title track. "For Youth" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. 

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS's sixth #1 on that chart. It became the best-selling album of 2022 in South Korea and fourth best-selling album of 2022 in the world. This album is currently the fourth best-selling album on South Korea of all time. 

As of January 2023, the album has sold 3,503,435 copies in South Korea alone.

## Records[]

Proof sold over two million copies worldwide on its release day. By the end of its first week, the album had sold a total of 2,752,496 copies, debuting at number one in several countries such as South Korea, Australia, Canada, and Japan, among others. In the United States, Proof became BTS' sixth consecutive number-one on the Billboard 200, with the most significant opening week for a group and the second-largest for an album released in the country in 2022.
