**Run Jin** (달려라 석진; also known as **Dallyeora Seokjin**) is a variety web series starring Jin. The series features Jin competing in games, challenges or activities with or without different guests each episode. 

The series aired on August 13, 2024 every Tuesday, after episode 11, _Run Jin_ was paused until December 3, 2024 due _Happy_ promotions. In episodes 15 and 16 the series was titled as **Run Hob-Jin** or **Dallyeora Hoseokjin** (달려라 호석진). 

Due to the series production ending, Jin suggested a concert as the finale, later was announced RUNSEOKJIN EP.TOUR world tour. The production ended on May 27, 2025. 

The series is a spin-off of _Run BTS!_.

## Trivia[]

  * Episode 16 was scheduled to be released on December 31, 2024 at 9:00pm KST but it was postponed as a sign of respect due to the Jeju Air Flight 2216 crash accident.
  * Jin worked again with multiple guests. 
    * Kim Dong-hyun and Lee Yi-kyung in _Handsome Guys_.
    * Jonathan Yiombi in _The K-Star Next Door 4_.
    * Kwaktube in _Kian's Bizarre B&B_.
    * Hong Jin-kyung in _Screwballs_.
  * Jin previously worked with multiple guests. 
    * Jee Seok-jin in _Running Man_.
    * Sandeul in _Half-Star Hotel in Lost Island_.
    * Shin Seung-ho and Oh Sang-uk in _Handsome Guys_.
    * Kian84 and Ji Ye-eun in _Kian's Bizarre B&B_.
