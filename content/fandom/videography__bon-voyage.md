**BTS 본보야지** (BTS: Bon Voyage) is a reality by the boy group BTS. The reality features the members of BTS travelling around the world doing different games or activities. 

The first season was available to be purchased on VLIVE, and was broadcasted weekly since July 5, 2016 until August 23, 2016. This season took place in Norway, Sweden and Finland for over 10 days, and it was a gift for their third anniversary. 

The second season was available to be purchased on VLIVE, and was broadcasted weekly since June 27, 2017 until August 15, 2017. This season took place in Hawaii for over 9 days. 

The third season was available to be purchased on VLIVE, and was released weekly since September 18, 2018 until November 6, 2018. This season took place in Malta, Europe. 

The four season was available to be purchased on Weverse, and was released weekly since aired on November 19, 2019 until January 7, 2020. This season took place in New Zealand.

## Trivia[]

### Season 1[]

  * Episode 0 is available without purchased the season.
  * The trip started on May 15, 2016.

### Season 2[]

  * This trip happened between the dates of The Wings Tour.
  * The concept of this trip is "Let's eat, have fun, and play".
  * During the live broadcast of the first episode V Live collapsed and the episode had to be retransmitted.
  * In the intro of the first episode, RM's previous stage name "Rap Monster" was misspelled as "Rap Moster".

### Season 3[]

  * Episode 0 is available without purchased the season.

### Season 4[]

  * Episode 0 is available without purchased the season.
  * Episode 2 and Episode 2 (Story Untold) was scheduled to be released on November 26, 2019 at 9:00pm KST and November 28, 2019 at 9:00pm KST respectively but it was cancelled as a sign of respect to Koo Hara's passing.
