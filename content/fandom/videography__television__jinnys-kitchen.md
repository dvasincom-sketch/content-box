**Jinny's Kitchen** (서진이네; also known as **Seojin's Korean Street Food**) is a reality show featuring Lee Seo-jin, Jung Yu-mi, Park Seo-joon, Choi Woo-shik and V, released since February 24, 2023 until May 5, 2023 on tvN. 

The series follows the story of Lee Seo-jin, who has finally gone from manager to full-on restaurant owner after years of working under Youn Yuh-jung at 'Youn's Kitchen' and 'Youn's Stay'.
