_**Love Yourself: Tear**_ (LOVE YOURSELF 轉 'Tear') is the third studio album by BTS. It was released on May 18, 2018 with "FAKE LOVE" serving as the album's title track. "Airplane Pt.2" and "Anpanman" were used as follow-up tracks during promotions. 

The album debuted at #1 on Billboard 200, becoming BTS' first #1 on that chart. 

The album was a nominee for the GRAMMYs "Best Recording Package". 

The album was the second best-selling album of 2018 in South Korea. This album is currently the seventh best-selling album on South Korea of all time. 

As 2022, the album has sold 3,066,245 copies only in South Korea.
