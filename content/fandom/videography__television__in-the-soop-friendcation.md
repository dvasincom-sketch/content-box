**In the Soop: Friendcation** (인더숲우정여행; also known as **In the Forest: Friendship Trip**) is a reality show featuring Park Seo-joon, Peakboy, Park Hyung-sik, Woo-shik and V, released since July 22, 2022 until August 12, 2022 on JTBC and Disney+. 

The series is a spinoff of In the SOOP BTS ver.
