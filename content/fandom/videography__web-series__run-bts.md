**Run BTS!** (달려라 방탄!; also known as **Dallyeora Bangtan!** or **DalBang!**) is a variety web series starring BTS, broadcasting intermittent for free viewing on Weverse and YouTube, was formerly offered weekly for free viewing on V LIVE. The series features the members of BTS in teams or individual competing in different games, challenges or activities. 

The first season aired on August 1, 2015 every Tuesday, unless an episode of _BTS Gayo_ was aired. After a one year break, a second season was aired on January 31, 2017, and since episode twenty-three, it became a weekly show due to the production of _BTS Gayo_ ending. 

On December 26, 2018, a trailer for season three was posted, announcing the premiere on January 1, 2019. After episode 85, _Run BTS!_ was paused to air BTS's docuseries _Bring The Soul_ , and was paused after episode 90 to air the season four of the reality web series _BTS: Bon Voyage_. 

On April 13, 2020, the intro for season three changed in line with the 100th episode and has since been in use. After episode 103, _Run BTS!_ was paused to air BTS' docuseries _Break The Silence_ , and was paused after episode 111 to air reality web series _In the SOOP BTS ver._

After episode 141, _Run BTS!_ was paused until June 15, 2021, after three episodes aired was paused again a month due _Butter_ promotions. On August 16, 2022, the show returned with ten special episodes released until February 21, 2023. 

Since V LIVE has been integrated into Weverse and the original service was completely terminated by December 31, 2022, all the episodes were uploaded on YouTube between December 21 to December 24, 2022. 

The web series was on hiatus due their mandatory military service from February 2023 until April 2026. On July 30, 2024, it was announced a spin-off titled _Run Jin_ that would being aired on August 13, 2024. 

On March 30, April 7 and April 20, 2026, teasers for a new season titled as **Run BTS! 2.0** (달려라 방탄 2.0) were posted, announcing the premiere on April 23, 2026.

## BTS Behind The Scenes[]

Alongside the main episodes, there are also behind the scenes episodes that can only be seen be seen with a BTS Channel Plus subscription. After the BTS Channel Plus was deleted at the beginning of 2020, all of the episodes became available to be purchased on Weverse. 

## Accolades[]

_Run BTS!_ was nominated for and won Best V Original at the 2018 V Live Awards. 

## Trivia[]

  * Episode 32 was scheduled to be released on December 19, 2017 at 9:00pm KST but it was cancelled as a sign of respect to SHINee's Jonghyun's passing.
  * Episode 87 was scheduled to be released on October 15, 2019 at 9:00pm KST but it was cancelled as a sign of respect to Sulli's passing.
  * Episode 102 was accidentally uploaded on April 22, 2020 at 4:32pm KST on Weverse with about 300 fans who viewed the episode. It was uploaded by mistake by a staff member and was deleted within 1 minute. The episode was supposed to be uploaded on April 28, 2020 at 9:00pm KST.
