**In the SOOP BTS ver.** (In the SOOP BTS편; also known as **BTS In the SOOP**) is a reality show by the boy group BTS. The reality features the members of BTS doing activities that are “somewhere in between everyday life and leisure.” 

The first season it was premiered on August 19, 2020, and was released every Wednesday at 11 p.m. KST on JTBC. An extended version of the series is available to be purchased on Weverse and was released every Thursday at 12 a.m. KST. Behind-the-scenes clips from the series are available by those who purchased the Weverse version. It were released every Tuesday at 9 p.m. KST. 

The second season it was premiered on August 15, 2021, and was released every Friday at 9 p.m. KST on JTBC. An extended version of the series is available to be purchased on Weverse and was released every Friday at 10 a.m. KST. Behind-the-scenes clips and a special episode from the series are available by those who purchased the Weverse version. 

The first season is the first season of the franchise, meanwhile the season two is the third.
