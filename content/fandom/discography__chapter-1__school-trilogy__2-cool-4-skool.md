## Trivia[]

  * "No More Dream" released on June 12, 2013 at 12:00am, in Korea; however, in the UK it released on June 11, 2013, at 15:00pm (3:00pm).
  * "We Are Bulletproof Pt.2" was released on July 17, 2013 at 12:00am, in Korea, however in the UK it was released on July 16, 2013, at 15:00pm (3:00pm).
  * This is BTS' first single.
  * This is BTS' only Korean single album
  * This single has two hidden tracks that do not appear on the album's tracklist.
