**BTS Episode** (also known as **EPISODE** ; stylized in all lower case) is a web show of behind the scenes videos featuring the members of BTS, unlike the Bangtan Bomb this videos are longer and show all their experience of that day, like is a jacket photo shooting, music videos shooting, concerts, awards shows, etc. 

Almost all the HYBE Labels artists have their own Episode videos in their channel or HYBE LABELS channel. There are also episodes called Big Hit Episode where the members appear with other artists of the company. 

Since 2020 on the 80th episode, they started to release them with English subtitles. 

After their hiatus due their mandatory military service, the episodes are uploaded without the '[Episode]' as part of the title and also the logo was removed, to know the episodes part of this web show it have to be uploaded to the official playlist and/or described as '[Episode]' in their official X.
