## Development[]

BT21 is the first project of LINE FRIENDS CREATORS. It is a new IP co-created by LINE FRIENDS and BTS. Rather than simply creating avatars of the physical appearance of artists, BT21 consists of 8 different charming characters created by BTS members, where all of them actively participated in the whole process from drawing sketches to elaboration.

## Trivia[]

  * BT21 is an idea made by Si-Hyuk Bang after the album Love Yourself: Her remains in the top 20 of the Billboard 200 four weeks after its release. 
  * BT21’s eight cartoonish characters were inspired by drawings created by the seven BTS members, with one correlating to each member and the final one representing the band and its fandom, ARMY.
  * The BT21 characters are gender-neutral.
  * The fandom name for BT21 is UNISTAR
