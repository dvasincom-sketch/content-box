_**The Most Beautiful Moment in Life: Young Forever**_ (화양연화 Young Forever; 花樣年華 Young Forever) is the first special album by BTS. It was released on May 2, 2016 with "불타오르네 (Fire)" serving as the album's title track. "Save Me" was used as a follow-up track during promotions. "Epilogue: Young Forever" serves also as the third single from their album. The album contains the tracks from their two previous mini albums, three new songs and several new remixes. 

The album was the fourth best-selling album of 2016 in South Korea.
