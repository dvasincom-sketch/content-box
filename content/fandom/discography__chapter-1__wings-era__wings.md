_**Wings**_ (stylized in all caps) is the second studio album by BTS. It was released on October 10, 2016 with "피 땀 눈물 (Blood Sweat & Tears)" serving as the album's title track. "Am I Wrong" and "21세기 소녀 (21st Century Girl)" were used as follow-up tracks during promotions. 

The album was the best-selling album of 2016 in South Korea. As of 2020, the album has sold more than one million copies alone and with the repackaged version more than two million copies.
