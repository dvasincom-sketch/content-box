_**Indigo**_ is the debut studio album by RM. It was released on December 2, 2022 with "Wild Flower" serving as the album's title track. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.

## Trivia[]

  * The album topped the iTunes Top Albums Charts in a total of 67 countries and regions. As December 2022, the album has sold 739,363 copies only in South Korea and it has been certified 2x Platinum on Circle Chart and KMCA.
