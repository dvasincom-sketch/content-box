_**MUSE**_ is the second mini album by Jimin. It was released on July 19, 2024 with "Who" serving as the album's title track. A pre-release track "Smeraldo Garden Marching Band (feat. Loco)" was released on June 28, 2024. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.
