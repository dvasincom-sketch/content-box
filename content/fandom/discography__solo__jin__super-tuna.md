"**Super Tuna** " (슈퍼 참치) is a song by Jin. Originally, the song was released on December 4, 2021 as an unofficially song. 

It was released officially on October 21, 2022 on global music platforms. 

The song was released in full version (previously showcased at the 2024 BTS Festa) featuring a new album cover on October 11, 2024, in some music platforms the short version was replaced by the full version.
