_**Love Yourself: Her**_ (LOVE YOURSELF 承 'Her') is the fifth mini album by BTS. It was released on September 18, 2017 with "DNA" serving as the album's title track. "MIC Drop" and "고민보다 Go (Go Go)" were used as follow-up tracks during promotions. 

The album debuted at #7 on Billboard 200. 

The album was the best-selling album of 2017 in South Korea. This album is currently the eighth best-selling album on South Korea of all time. 

As 2022, the album has sold 2,973,831 copies only in South Korea.
