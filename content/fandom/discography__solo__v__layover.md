_**Layover**_ is the debut mini album by V. It was released on September 8, 2023 with "Slow Dancing" serving as the album's title track. Two pre-release tracks "Love Me Again" and "Rainy Days" were released on August 11, 2023. 

This album is part of the Solo Debuts and the BTS Chronicle.

## Trivia[]

  * Hanteo Chart announced that _Layover_ sold 1,672,138 copies on September 8 alone, breaking the record for the highest first-day sales of any solo album in Hanteo history. The album sold 2,101,974 copies in its first week of its release, breaking the highest first-week sales of any solo album in Hanteo history.
  * Big Hit Music announced that _Layover_ sold 221,491 copies in Japan alone on the first day of its release, reaching #1 on the Oricon's daily album chart. _Layover_ hit #1 on the iTunes Top Albums charts in 65 different regions.
