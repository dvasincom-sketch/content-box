_**The Most Beautiful Moment in Life Pt.1**_ (화양연화 Pt.1; 花樣年華 Pt.1) is the third mini album by BTS. It was released on April 29, 2015, with "I Need U" serving as the album's title track. "쩔어 (Dope)" was used as a follow-up track during promotions and is the second single from the album. 

The album was the sixth best-selling album of 2015 in South Korea.

## Trivias[]

  * "I Need U" was released on April 30, 2015 at 12:00 am in Korea, however in the UK it was released on April 29, 2015 at 15:00 pm (3:00 pm).
  * "Dope" was released on June 24, 2015 at 12:00 am in Korea, however in the UK it was released on June 23, 2015 at 15:00 pm (3:00 pm).
  * This is BTS's 3rd mini album.
